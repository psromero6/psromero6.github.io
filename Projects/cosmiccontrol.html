Cosmic Control

A mobile game for Android which puts you in control of a space station and the traffic around it. As the station commander it is your job to guide increasing numbers of ships into the station while avoiding collisions and destroying oncoming asteroids.

My Role in this project was general Development and implementaion of new technologies. I integrated it to the Google Play Store with leaderboards, statistics, ads, and purchasing. I worked on nearly all of the game logic. I also had a hand in the design of the game near the end of the project.

This was the first professional title that I worked on. I have been working at a senior level, managing code reviews, source control, and releases while activly developing new features, and polishing existing ones, while we move from closed to open beta.
